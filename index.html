Offtie

Allows users to save pages from URLs in Twitter timeline for offline reading.

Welcome to Offtie official page.

Thank you for interesting in iPhone app "Offtie". This app is userful for travelers which rely on Twitter as their source of information. Offtie is used to view URLs from your Twitter timeline when you do not have Internet connection. When you have Wi-Fi or cellular coverage - download twitter timeline using Offtie. Once you are far from Internet - just open the app and read all the pages posted to you timeline. For example: before your board plane download timeline in Offtie. When you are in the air - open Offtie again and read all missed tweets with downloaded pages they refer.

FAQ

  1. Question: Does Offtie require internet connection to load pages? Answer: No. Once the timeline is saved you do not have to be connected to internet to load pages from tweets.
  2. Question: Does Offtie posts tweets on my behalf?

    Answer: No. Offtie does not post tweets at all.

  3. Question: What is average amount of traffic used to download timeline?

    Answer: About 2Mb for 20 tweets.

  4. Question: Does Offtie saves images/videos from pages?

    Answer: No. Offtie saves only text from the page.

  5. Question: If I use Offtie to view downloaded pages - does it still connect to Internet?

    Answer: If internet connection is available - Offtie will try to load images from the page. If Internet connection is not available - only text from the page will be loaded to user.

  6. Question Does Offtie support multiple Twitter accounts?

    Answer: Yes. Whatever amount of accounts you have added to your iPhone - all of them can be used by Offtie.
